* w/o PRV & PLD (3 points only — 0%, 100%, 150%): Set PRV to max working pressure and disconnect PLD. Record readings at 0%, 100%, 150% flow only.
** w/ PLD Enabled (all 7 points): Enable PLD and record all 7 flow points. Then run at churn while setting PLD to 175 psi. PRV shall be ≥10 psi above PLD setting.
