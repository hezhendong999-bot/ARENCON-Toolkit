Integrated Testing Notes
Ongoing Integrated Systems Testing

As required by CAN/ULC-S1001, Integrated Testing shall be completed at five-year intervals after the initial test. Where modifications to fire protection and life safety systems affect system integrations, Integrated Systems Testing of the affected integrations shall be conducted.
